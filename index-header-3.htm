About Me

Johnny Minor has been a photographer since when he picked up his first camera at the age of 3 and told his Mum "I'm going to be the next Ansel Adams!" Since then Johnny has been voraciously appraoching many forms of photography ranging from landscape to street to portrait. He has obliged such outstanding corporations with his fine skill such as National Geographic, Patagonia, Outdoor Photography, United States National Parks, Formula 1, 3M, and Google.
